About Korean Quest

Mission: make Korean learning engaging, collaborative, and accessible through gamified lessons and peer-to-peer support.

How We Meet FBLA Requirements

  • Schedule page: live tutoring & group study with RSVP (see Schedule).
  • Student dashboard: track XP, streaks, badges, RSVPs (see Dashboard).
  • Resources: lessons, videos, quizzes, downloadables (see Resources).

Accessibility

  • Semantic headings, labeled controls, and ARIA live regions for dynamic content.
  • Keyboard-reachable buttons and visible focus styles via CSS.
  • Color contrast and non-color cues (icons + labels) planned in CSS.

Attribution

Any third-party videos or assets will be cited on the Resources page. This demo uses student-written content.

Team

  • Curriculum & Content — Student Authors
  • Design & Front-End — Student Designers
  • Testing & QA — Study Group Volunteers
